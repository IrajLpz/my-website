Our History

Nita's Coffee started in early August 2019 during a pandemic. Owned and founded by Mrs. Genesis Picar Macalipay. It was a big risk to take, especially for first-time business owners. Nonetheless, our founders took the chance to establish a coffee business that soon gained popularity among the neighborhood. It is without a doubt that the growth of Nita's Coffee is remarkable, as we carefully select premium ingredients and ensure every cup meets high-quality standards at a fair price.

We spent months perfecting our signature blend, taste-testing 37 different bean varieties until we discovered the perfect balance of boldness and smoothness. Those early days saw us delivering thermoses of experimental brews to our friends, families, and neighbors at dawn, quietly gathering handwritten feedback under doorways before the morning rush.

Our first small store operated from a house terrace in a town house village in Sta.Elena Antipolo City during a record-breaking pandemic then now officially expanded & relocated in a bigger area near the Antipolo Cathedral. Regulars still smile remembering how we kept warm by the heat of the roaster, serving cups through a makeshift window carved in the wooden planks. About Us

Nita's Coffee opened and started running on August, 2019 during the pandemic. Started in a mini shop/store in a town house village, then expanded and relocated during 2024.
