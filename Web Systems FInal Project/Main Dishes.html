BEST SELLING DISHES/CUSTOMERS FAVORITE

Porksilog

A satisfying Filipino breakfast dish, featuring tender and flavorful pork, garlic-infused fried rice, and a perfectly cooked fried egg. The combination of textures and flavors creates a delightful culinary experience. The tender pork adds a rich and savory element, while the garlic-infused fried rice provides a aromatic base. Topped with a perfectly cooked fried egg, the runny yolk adds a creamy element that ties everything together.
Php 130.00

The combination of textures and flavors creates a delightful culinary experience. The crispy fried chicken adds a satisfying crunch, while the garlic-infused fried rice provides a rich and aromatic base. Topped with a perfectly cooked fried egg, the runny yolk adds a creamy element that ties everything together.

Mushroom Sisig Marinara Pasta

A unique fusion dish, blending the savory flavors of Filipino sisig with the richness of Italian marinara sauce and the earthiness of mushrooms. This innovative combination creates a bold and satisfying meal. The sisig, typically made with minced pork's face, is reimagined with mushrooms for a vegetarian twist, adding a meaty texture without the meat. The marinara sauce brings a tangy and slightly sweet element, complementing the umami flavors of the mushrooms.
Php 135.00

Sisig Platter

About Us

Nita's Coffee opened and started running on August, 2019 during the pandemic. Started in a mini shop/store in a town house village, then expanded and relocated during 2024. A unique coffee shop with both reggae and cozy vibes. Their quality coffee drinks makes their shop distinct with others. Soup dishes are also an option but only for those who like soups. Having a band playing while enjoying your coffee and snacks are truly a masterpiece of their coffee shop, which tend to attract more couples and students to spend their leisure time inside the coffee shop.
