About Us

Nita's Coffee opened and started running on August, 2019 during the pandemic. Started in a mini shop/store in a town house village, then expanded and relocated during 2024. A unique coffee shop with both reggae and cozy vibes. Their quality coffee drinks makes their shop distinct with others. Japanese dishes are also an option in their menu for those who like soups. Having a band playing while enjoying your coffee and snacks are truly a masterpiece of their coffee shop, which tend to attract more couples and students to spend their leisure time inside the coffee shop.
